    Track your heart rate whenever you want

    Follow Your Heart is a mobile app that makes it easy to monitor your heart rate directly from your device. Offering real-time insights, it's perfect for anyone looking to enhance cardiovascular health, manage stress, or optimize workouts.

    01

    Visualise your heart rate

    A visualised heart rate can makes you better understanding on your exercise performance.

    • Read the real-time heart rate without waiting
    • Understand if it's under normal level
    • Quick change mode to exercise

    Hourly Heart Rate Overview

    View your heart rate changes over the past hour in this simple graph. Hover over the points to see specific heart rate values at each minute.

    02

    • Allows quick and easy interpretation of heart rate trends over time
    • Seeing fluctuations can motivate users to make healthier choices or adjust their activity levels

    Personalize Your Experience

    Customize your profile settings by entering your gender, age, and weight. This information helps tailor the app's recommendations and insights to better suit your health and fitness goals. Update your details anytime to keep your profile accurate and relevant.

    03

    • Recommendations to the individual's specific health and fitness needs.
    • Track changes and improvements over time as they update their weight or age
